1.0 Technical & Legal Glossary

This document defines the standardized terminology used across the SALA Risk Engine and compliance reports.

1.0 BIOMECHANICAL METRICS

1.1 Ri (Risk Index): A dynamic value (0-100%) quantifying the probability of musculoskeletal injury based on real-time telemetry.
1.2 L5/S1 Compression: The mechanical force exerted on the intervertebral disc between the 5th lumbar vertebra and the sacrum during lifting.
1.3 Δf (Fatigue Coefficient): The mathematical rate of muscle degradation relative to shift duration and previous workload.

2.0 OPERATIONAL STANDARDS

2.1 CoF (Coefficient of Friction): Measurement of surface stability. Critical threshold for ice operations is < 0.1.
2.2 WBV (Whole-Body Vibration): Mechanical vibration exposure during vehicle operation, measured under ISO 2631-1 standards.

3.0 LEGAL COMPLIANCE

3.1 AML (Arbeidsmiljøloven): The Norwegian Working Environment Act, which dictates legal limits for exertion, rest, and safety protocols.
3.2 Mechanical Mandate: A compulsory operational shift to mechanical-only handling when $Ri$ exceeds 75%.
