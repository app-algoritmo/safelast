Technical Note: SALA-037-EN

Subject: Integration of the 37.5-hour work week into risk models.

1. Objective

To align SALA's real-time risk index ($R_i$) with the Norwegian standard 37.5-hour work week, ensuring fatigue multipliers are legally compliant.

2. Fatigue Modeling

The system applies a linear degradation scale to the stabilizer muscles of the lower back:

Fatigue_Factor (Fi) = (Hours_Worked / 37.5) * 1.25

3. Compliance Protocol

If the cumulative $F_i$ exceeds 0.85 within a single week, the system triggers a "Mechanical Only" mandate for the driver to prevent long-term musculoskeletal injury.
