Technical Note: SALA-037-EN

Subject: Integration of the 37.5-hour work week into risk models.

1. Objective

To align SALA's real-time risk index ($R_i$) with the Norwegian standard 37.5-hour work week, ensuring fatigue multipliers are legally compliant.

2. Fatigue Modeling

The system applies a linear degradation scale to the stabilizer muscles of the lower back (L5/S1 region):

$$F_i = \left( \frac{\text{Hours Worked}}{37.5} \right) \times 1.25$$

3. Compliance Protocol

If the cumulative $F_i$ exceeds 0.85 within a single week, the system triggers a "Mechanical Only" mandate for the driver.
