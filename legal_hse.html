⚖️ HSE & Legal Compliance Documentation

Information for Arbeidstilsynet and internal Safety Delegates (Verneombud).

1. Regulatory Alignment

SALA satisfies the monitoring requirements of Arbeidsmiljøloven § 4-4 regarding the physical work environment.

2. Data Privacy (GDPR)

Biomechanical data is anonymized and used strictly for injury prevention, not for individual performance tracking.
